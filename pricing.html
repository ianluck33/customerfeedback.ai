Pricing FAQ

Questions about pricing,
answered honestly.

Is the free plan actually free, or is it a trial?

+
The Free plan is genuinely free forever — no credit card required, no time limit, no trial that expires. You get 500 responses per month, 3 surveys, and AI setup from your URL, permanently. We're not playing bait-and-switch; the Free plan is how we build trust with teams before they're ready to pay.

What is "founding member pricing" and why does it matter?

+
Founding members lock in today's pricing forever. As we add features and increase pricing for new users, your rate stays the same. We're in early access right now — this is the cheapest CustomerFeedback.ai will ever be. If you join during early access, you get the lowest price we'll ever charge, for as long as you remain a customer.

Can I switch plans at any time?

+
Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we prorate the charge. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing period. No penalties, no lock-ins, no questions asked.

What happens if I go over my response limit?

+
On the Free plan, we'll notify you when you're approaching 500 responses and pause new collection until the next month or until you upgrade. On paid plans, there's no hard limit — Starter and above include unlimited responses. We won't bill you overages or cut you off without warning.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

+
Yes — annual billing saves 30% vs. monthly. Starter drops from $69/month to $49/month billed annually. Growth drops from $199/month to $149/month billed annually. We recommend annual if you know you'll be using CustomerFeedback.ai long-term — it's the biggest discount we offer.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or early-stage startups?

+
Yes. Registered nonprofits receive 40% off paid plans. Early-stage startups (pre-revenue or under $1M ARR) can apply for our startup program which includes Growth plan features at Starter pricing. Email hello@customerfeedback.ai with your details and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
