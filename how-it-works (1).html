FAQ

Common questions
answered honestly.

Does AI really build the entire program from just my URL?

+
Yes. Our AI reads your public website to extract brand voice, industry signals, product type, and customer profile. It uses this to generate tailored survey questions, recommend the right feedback methodology (NPS vs CSAT vs CES), set cadence rules, and configure follow-up logic. You review and launch — nothing more required.

What if my website doesn't have much content?

+
If your site is sparse or in stealth mode, the AI will ask you a few quick questions about your industry, customer type, and goals. Takes about 90 seconds. You'll still be live in under 5 minutes.

Can I customize what the AI generates?

+
Absolutely. Everything the AI creates is a starting point, not a black box. You can edit every question, change the tone, adjust the cadence, add or remove touchpoints, and configure logic rules — all through a clean visual editor. Most users find they change very little because the AI gets it right on the first pass.

How does the "closed-loop" automation work?

+
When a detractor responds, AI immediately drafts a personalized recovery email based on their specific comment, alerts your team in Slack, and creates a follow-up task. For promoters, it can request a review or testimonial automatically. For at-risk accounts, it surfaces the alert to your CS team with the full account context. You approve or send with one click.

Do I need to involve my dev team?

+
For email and link-based surveys — never. For in-app surveys (embedded widget in your product), there's a one-time JavaScript snippet install, equivalent in complexity to adding Google Analytics. Most teams add it themselves in under 10 minutes. We provide code and documentation, and our support team is available if needed.

What integrations are available out of the box?

+
HubSpot, Salesforce, Intercom, Zendesk, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zapier, and 40+ more. All integrations are configured with a few clicks — no engineering work required. Enterprise plans include custom webhook and API access for bespoke integrations.
